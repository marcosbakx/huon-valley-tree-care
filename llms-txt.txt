# Huon Valley Tree Care

## About
Huon Valley Tree Care is a professional arborist business based in southern Tasmania, Australia. Owned and operated by Marcos Bakx, who holds an AQF Level 5 qualification in Arboriculture — the highest trade-level qualification in the industry. The business has over 12 years of experience and is fully insured.

## Services
- Tree Removal and Felling
- Tree Pruning
- Habitat Creation
- Cable Bracing
- Tree Maintenance

## Service Areas
Huon Valley (including Huonville, Ranelagh, Franklin, Cygnet, Geeveston, Dover, Southport, Glen Huon, Grove, Mountain River, Port Huon and surrounding localities), Kingborough (including Kingston, Kingston Beach, Blackmans Bay, Taroona, Margate, Snug, Kettering, Woodbridge and surrounding localities), and Hobart's southern suburbs (including Sandy Bay, South Hobart, Dynnyrne, Fern Tree, Mount Nelson, Tolmans Hill, Ridgeway).
