About Huon Valley Tree Care

Huon Valley Tree Care is owned and operated by Marcos Bakx, a qualified arborist holding an AQF Level 5 in Arboriculture. With over 12 years of hands-on experience, Marcos brings the knowledge and care needed to look after your trees properly.

Whether it's a complex removal in a tight space, careful pruning to improve the health of a mature tree, or creating habitat for native wildlife, Marcos takes pride in doing the job right. Fully insured and locally based, Huon Valley Tree Care is a business built on reputation, word of mouth, and a genuine love for the work.

Our Services

From routine maintenance to complex removals, we provide a full range of professional tree services across southern Tasmania.

Tree Removal & Felling

Safe, controlled removal of dangerous, dead, or unwanted trees. We handle everything from small backyard trees to large-scale removals in difficult access areas, with full clean-up included.

Tree Pruning

Expert pruning to improve tree health, shape, and safety. We follow Australian standards for crown reduction, deadwooding, canopy lifting, and formative pruning to keep your trees looking their best.

Habitat Creation

Thoughtful modification of trees to create hollows, nesting sites, and habitat features for native wildlife. A specialised service that supports Tasmania's unique biodiversity while managing your property.

Cable Bracing

Installation of support systems to stabilise structurally compromised trees. Cable bracing can extend the safe life of a tree by reducing the risk of limb or trunk failure during storms and high winds.

Tree Maintenance

Ongoing care to keep your trees healthy and your property safe. Regular inspections, minor pruning, storm damage clean-up, and general tree health assessments tailored to your property's needs.
